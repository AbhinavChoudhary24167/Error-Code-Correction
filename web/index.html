What is happening & why (theory quick map)

This dashboard mirrors the repository workflow: reliability and SER models estimate FIT, energy/carbon models estimate efficiency impact, and the selector reports Pareto-optimal ECC choices. Use these notes as a fast onboarding guide.

1) Reliability lens ⓘ

Lower FIT means stronger resilience, but may require stricter scrub/recovery policy.

2) Carbon & energy lens ⓘ

Lower carbon can reduce operational burden, but can shift reliability or latency behavior.

3) Pareto lens ⓘ

Each candidate is a trade-off: improve one objective and another may worsen.
