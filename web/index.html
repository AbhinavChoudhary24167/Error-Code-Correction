Help & interpretation guide

Reliability lens

Lower FIT means stronger resilience, usually with performance/energy trade-offs.

Carbon & energy lens

Lower carbon can reduce operational burden, but may impact other objectives.

Pareto lens

Pareto points are non-dominated candidates across reliability, carbon, and latency.

All data comes from repository artifacts configured in web/datasets.json.
